📊 Atliq Hardware Sales & Market Analytics (Excel Project)
Turning fragmented Excel data into business insights
🚀 Overview

Atliq Hardware is a B2B company that manufactures and sells computer hardware (PCs, peripherals, etc.) through multiple customers, regions, and sales channels.

Like many real-world organizations, its data is spread across multiple Excel files, making reporting slow, manual, and difficult to scale.

This project focuses on transforming that fragmented data into structured, decision-ready reports using Excel.

🧠 Business Understanding

Atliq operates through a layered business model:

Customers (B2B) → Retailers & platforms (not end consumers)
Platforms
Brick & Mortar (physical stores)
E-commerce platforms
Sales Channels
Retailer → Third-party stores
Direct → Atliq’s own stores & e-store
Distributor → Region-based distribution partners

Understanding this structure was critical to avoid misinterpreting the data (especially customer vs consumer).

⚠️ Problem Statement
Data scattered across multiple Excel files
No centralized reporting system
Heavy manual effort in analysis
Limited visibility into:
Customer performance
Market performance
Target vs Actual sales
🎯 Project Objective

To build structured Excel reports that provide clear insights into:

Customer performance
Market performance
Financial performance (P&L)
📊 Reports Created
📌 1. Customer Performance Report
Net Sales by customer (2019–2021)
Year-over-Year (YoY) growth analysis
Performance comparison across customers

👉 Helps identify:

High-value customers
Fast-growing accounts
Underperforming segments
🌍 2. Market Performance Report
Country-wise Net Sales
Target vs Actual comparison (2021)
Performance gap analysis

👉 Helps identify:

Top-performing markets
Markets missing targets
Expansion opportunities
💰 3. P&L (Profit & Loss) Report
Net Sales
Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
Gross Margin
Gross Margin %

👉 Helps track:

Profitability trends
Cost efficiency
Margin performance over time

🛠️ Tools & Techniques Used

Microsoft Excel (Advanced)
Pivot Tables
SUMIFS, XLOOKUP, IF logic
Data Cleaning & Transformation
Basic ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) concepts
Business Analysis & Reporting

📊 Key Insights

Total sales grew significantly, reaching ~598M in 2021, indicating strong business expansion
India emerged as a top-performing market, contributing the highest revenue
Many markets missed their 2021 targets, highlighting gaps in forecasting or execution
Several customers showed 300%+ YoY growth, indicating high-value partnerships
Despite revenue growth, Gross Margin % declined slightly, suggesting rising costs

💼 Business Impact

Enables identification of high-performing customers and markets
Provides clear visibility into target vs actual performance
Supports better decision-making for:
Market expansion
Cost optimization
Customer prioritization

💡 Key Highlights

Translated business requirements into structured analytical reports
Worked with realistic, messy Excel-based data
Focused on decision-making, not just reporting
Built reports aligned with real-world business use cases
